Best running routes in Ireland

The Marker Route


This is a popular urban running route that takes you along the scenic Grand Canal and the picturesque Dublin Docklands. The route starts at The Marker Hotel and follows the canal path, offering stunning views of the water and the city skyline. It's a flat and fast route, perfect for runners of all levels.


The Phoenix Route


The Phoenix Park route is a favorite among local runners, offering a mix of scenic beauty and challenging terrain. The route takes you through one of Europe's largest urban parks, with plenty of greenery, wildlife, and historic landmarks to admire along the way. The park features a variety of trails, from flat and easy paths to more hilly and technical routes, making it suitable for runners of all levels.
